Welcome to my personal homepage! or mainly as a resume.
I am a Senior Engineer Staff Member at Tongji University, Shanghai China. I graduated in August 2002 with a Ph.D. from the Research Institute of Traffic Information Engineering & Control at Tongji University supervised by Prof. Shujing Zhang. The thesis title is "Research on Analytical Models of TCP Congestion Control". I am now teaching the courses of 'Computer Networks & Control Technology', 'Computer Control Technology' and 'Modern Electronics'.
